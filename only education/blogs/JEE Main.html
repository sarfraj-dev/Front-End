75 Percent Criteria for JEE Mains 2024

Last year, the NTA reintroduced the 75 percent marks qualifying condition in JEE Mains. Candidates who want to enroll into their selected institute must now score at least 75% in their class 12th to qualify for JEE Mains. There was no such criteria before to COVID-19, and no 12th % was necessary for JEE Mains. Any applicant deemed unfit under the JEE Mains 75 percent criteria would be declared disqualified and will not be admitted to institutes such as IITs, NITs, IIITs, and CFTIs.
